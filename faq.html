ABOUT COGNITIVE IMPAIRMENT

Our assessment platform is designed to benefit individuals who may be experiencing cognitive changes or have concerns about their cognitive health. This includes patients seeking early detection of cognitive decline, caregivers looking for ways to support their loved ones, and healthcare professionals involved in diagnosis and treatment planning.
